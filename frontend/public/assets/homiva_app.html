Safety Architecture

6-Layer Verification System

Our safety is not just a feature — it's our core USP and competitive moat.

1

KYC Verification

Government ID verification (Aadhaar, PAN) confirming identity before joining the platform.

2

Police Verification

Criminal background check to ensure workers are safe to operate in family homes.

3

Training & Screening

Category-specific training — elder care, babysitting, household help, or pet care.

4

Platform Certification

Official HOMIVA certificate post training — proving quality and professional credibility.

5

Dual OTP Authentication

Start & end OTPs ensure every service begins and ends with customer confirmation.

6

Real-Time GPS Tracking

Customer tracks the service partner live throughout the booking duration.
